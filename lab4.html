Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 slices cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat skillet on meduim heat
  2. Spread butter on both slices of bread
  3. Lay bread butter side down on skillet
  4. Top with a slice of cheese, 2 slices tomato, bacon, and another slice of cheese
  5. Cover with a slice of bread, butter side out
  6. Fry sandwiches until golden on both sides
